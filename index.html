My Start

What’s up? It’s Chris, aka LilChrisProductionz, aka LuhChrisBeatz. I started making beats in December, 2016 and really have improved a lot so far. Making beats changed my career tremendously. I wanted to be a neurologist, then a game designer. As you can see both of these careers do not have anything to do with making beats. It seems like music is all around me. When I hear a cool noise, I make something in my head. Music and producing is the best thing that happened to me.

Goals for Producing

I plan to be the biggest producer out of Memphis and biggest producer overall. I don’t doubt it all, I know it is going to happen one day. Later in my career I plan to complete a new genre. I am trying to combine two genres together to make something special. Again, I know it is going to happen because I’m very dedicated to music. I’m still learning things about producing, but it won’t be long until I master it.
